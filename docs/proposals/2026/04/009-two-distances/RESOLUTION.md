# Resolution: Proposal 009 — Two Distances

**Date:** 2026-04-10
**Decision:** ACCEPTED

## Designers' verdict

Both designers accepted unanimously.

**Hickey:** TP is a place masquerading as a value — frozen at entry while
step 3c provides liveness. Runner-trail complects portfolio state with
market state by conditioning a market-state function on phase. Two
distances answer two independent questions without entanglement. "The
system gets simpler and loses no information. That is the only reliable
signal that you are removing the right thing."

**Beckman:** The four cascades are structurally independent parallel copies
of the same diagram — removing two narrows the product type but cannot
break the commuting diagram. TP is a constant morphism dominated by
trail's dynamic one. Runner-trail learns the same function as trail
(phase is not in the thought vector) — redundant basis vector, not a
degree of freedom. "Half the surface area, same algebraic rank."

## The decision

Two distances: **trail** and **stop**.

- Trail: how much reversal to tolerate. The profit mechanism.
- Stop: how much adverse movement to survive. The loss mechanism.

Take-profit dissolves. Runner-trail dissolves. The runner phase persists
— it marks "principal is covered." The trail continues to breathe via
step 3c. The distance doesn't switch at the runner transition — the
reckoner already adapts to the current market context through the
composed thought.

## Empirical risk

Both designers flagged: if the thought doesn't encode enough trending-
regime signal, the trail reckoner might predict tight trails during
runners. The 100k benchmark answers this. If Grace degrades, the
information was in the extra fibers and they return.

- Simulation: two functions, not four
