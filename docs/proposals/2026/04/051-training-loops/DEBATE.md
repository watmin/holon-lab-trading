# Debate: Proposal 051 — Training Loops

Five voices reviewed. Three themes converged:

1. **Direction contaminates distance learning.** The position
   observer is blamed for market observer failures. Every voice
   agrees: decouple them. But HOW?

2. **Self-referential grading creates limit cycles.** The rolling
   median tracks the learner. The Red Queen. But replace with WHAT?
   - Seykota: hindsight-optimal distances (absolute)
   - Wyckoff: phase capture ratio (market-derived)
   - Beckman: freeze the threshold or abandon binary grading
   - Van Tharp: decouple from direction accuracy entirely

3. **The broker's composed thought is dead computation.** The
   reckoner was stripped. Nobody learns from the composition.
   Restore it? Remove it? Redesign it?

## For the debaters
